What Are Drones?

A drone is an aircraft that can operate without a person on board to control it; They are more formally known as Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs). Drones can come in all sorts of types, shapes and sizes:

FPV Racing Drone

Photography Drone

Search And Rescue Drone

They are becoming increasingly popular in modern times because of the vast uses they have. Drones can be used for aerial photography, search and rescue operations, geographic mapping, building safety inspections, express shipping, or even just as a fun hobby. It is no secret that there is controversy around privacy and drones, but if used with good intentions and properly, this continuously evolving technology can benefit all of us.

We founded this high school club in 2016 in hopes of sharing our love for this hobby and knowledge of drones with other students. Since then, we have run countless meetings, teaching students of any grade and skill level about how drones work, how to build them and how to fly them safely. Our club consists of 20+ members and we have tons of drone equipment for students to use. If you are interested in joining sign up here!
